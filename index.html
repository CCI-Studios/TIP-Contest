What is the
Toenail Fungus
Information Program (TIP)?

The Toenail Fungus Information Program (TIP) is a free, non-commercial educational resource that provides medically accurate, up to date information on the causes, preventative measures, symptoms, diagnosis and treatment options related to toenail fungus infections.

Sign up to TIP and you will receive updates on local or online events such as webinars with experts in dermatology, podiatry and other related specialties; information material, research, questions to ask your doctor and helpful tips on footcare.

Click here to sign up to receive information from TIP.

You may unsubscribe from TIP at any time. Please check the Privacy Policy.
